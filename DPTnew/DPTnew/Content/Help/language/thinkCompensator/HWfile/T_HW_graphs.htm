Displaying Graphs in Compensator

In most Compensator commands, namely the following:

  • Identify Displacement Field
  • Clean Displacement Field
  • Compute Distances

A Type drop-down list is available under the Graph node. It enables you to choose one of the following options:

Distribution To create a distribution graph, thus displaying the number of points with a particular value.


 
Repartition To create a repartition graph, thus displaying the total amount of points below a particular tolerance (the sum starting from value 0).


 

Graphs can be very useful, for example, to check that the optimization actually improved your results.
