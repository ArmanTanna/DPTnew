If you do not have a personal account yet to access the Customer Care's Web site, you can get it in one of the following ways.

  • Apply to your company primary contact (the persons in your company who are in charge of creating new users for think3 Customer Care). They can obtain up to one personal account for each of the users of think3 products.
  • Connect to the Customer Care's Web site (http://care.think3.com) and click Access Issues form. Now fill in the form and press Submit to forward your request to the Customer care team.
