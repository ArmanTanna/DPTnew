HelpThinkDesign


 

The product help command activates the relevant think3 product on-line help.

The help is designed to be used as a reference tool in your everyday work with the think3 product. In addition to complete descriptions of how commands work, it includes many step-by-step training examples.

The think3 product help window is composed of two frames. The frame on the right-hand side contains descriptions, while the frame on the left-hand side, the Navigation Frame, helps you locate information. This frame includes fourthree tabs — Contents, Index, Search and Favorites — enabling you to locate the information you are seeking using the most convenient method.
