InsertPointOn Intersection of Curves
 
 In the 3D Environment (Model):
The On Intersection of Curves/Surface/Mesh command draws points on intersections between curves or between curves and a surface or between curves and a mesh.
In the 2D Environment (Drawing):
The On Intersection of Curves command draws points on intersections between curves.
