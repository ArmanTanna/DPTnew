ModifyDrawing ViewDelete Breaks on View/Section


 

The Delete Breaks on View/Section command enables you to delete the breaks (created using any Break Lines command) on a view or section.

The command is also available in the drawing environment from the context menu of broken views or sections and Drawing Structure.


Note
The Undo command does not apply to Delete Breaks on View/Section. If you delete the breaks you can't get them back again by undoing your action.
