Importance of selection points in the Entities mode of Trim/Extend Curves with Limits

In the Trim/Extend Curves with Limits command, when the Boundaries mode is set to Entities, in case of multiple intersections between the boundaries and the curve to be modified, the results you will obtain depend on the positions at which you click to select them:

  • The actual intersection point that will be taken into account is the one closest to the selection point of the boundary.
  • The selection point of the curve defines the part of it that will be retained (that is: not removed or extended).
