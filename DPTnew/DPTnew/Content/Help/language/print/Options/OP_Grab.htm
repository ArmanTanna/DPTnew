Prints an area inside a box defined by indicating two opposite corners of it. The grabbing operation takes place as soon as you click the Print button. The cursor is changed to the one displayed below:



To select the area to print:

  1. Click on the first corner of the window you want to grab and hold down the mouse button.
  2. Drag it until you reach the right position for the second corner.
  3. Release the button to grab the window.
