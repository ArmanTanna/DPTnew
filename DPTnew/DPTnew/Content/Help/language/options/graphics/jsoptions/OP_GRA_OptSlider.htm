Enables you to set the representation level of model Drawing details during panning , zooming and rotations operations. The slider is active and accessible only if the Quality/speed check box is selected.

As per the slider setting the program dynamically determines the framerate. When you set the slider to Full detail, during dynamic movements, the graphics quality is not reduced. That is, the program will automatically set the graphics at a higher quality possible. This may result in a drop in performance, in the case of heavy models files.

When the slider is pushed to Fast, the graphics quality is reduced during translations, resulting in a better performance , even with heavy models. Of course, after the translation operation the model drawing is displayed in full quality.


To change the detail representation level, just drag the slider to the corresponding tick. Moving from left to right, you can decrease the quality (thus increasing speed) from Full detail to Fast, assigning all the values corresponding to the intermediate ticks.

Full detail: All details — i.e.: all objects, no matter how small they are — will be displayed during panning , zooming and rotations of the model. This setting may slow operations down.

Fast: Details — i.e.: small objects — will be displayed using a point rather than their real representation during panning , zooming and rotations of the model. This setting speeds operations up as much as possible.
