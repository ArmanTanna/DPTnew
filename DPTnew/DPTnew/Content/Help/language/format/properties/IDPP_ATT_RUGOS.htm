Entity Properties

Category: Dimension-Symbol-Surface Textures

All around

All around

Enables you to include an circle to the surface texture symbol, indicating that the surface texture is to go all around the surfces of a work piece.

  • When selected, the program adds a circle to the surface texture symbol.
  • When not selected, circle is not added to the symbol.

Line

Line

The controls in this area enable you to specify line settings for the surface texture symbol.

Tangent witness line

Tangent witness line

Enables you to display the surface texture symbol along with a witness line, that behaves as a tangent to the curved entities. This option is controlled by the check box provided:

  • When selected, the surface texture symbol is displayed along with the Tangent witness line.
  • When not selected, the surface texture symbol is displayed normally.

Leader line

Leader line

Enables you to attach a Leader line to the surface texture symbol. This option is controlled by the check box provided:

  • When selected, a Leader line is attached to the surface texture symbol.
  • When not selected, no Leader line is attached.

Orientation

Vertical

Enables you to determine the Orientation of the surface texture symbol. You have the following options:

Horizontal Enables you to display the surface texture symbol in the Horizontal direction.
Vertical Enables you to display the surface texture symbol in the Vertical direction.
