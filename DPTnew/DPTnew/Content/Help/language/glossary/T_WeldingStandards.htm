Welding standards

The think3 product enables you to manage the following welding standards:

  • ISO (ISO 2553)
  • ANSI (ANSI/AWS A2.4)
  • JIS (Z 3021)
  • GB (GBT 12212)
  • KS (KS B 0052)

The welding standard you choose determines the welding symbols available and their names. Use the Weldings command to set the standard of your choice.
