Trimming a surface

After starting the Trim Both Surfaces command, proceed as follows.

1 Select the surface to be used as the limit.



To change the selected limit surface, right-click on Limit Surface, choose Reset in the context menu and select another surface.

2 Select the surface(s) to be cut.



To change the selected surfaces, right-click, select Reset Current Selection and select other surfaces.
End selection by right-clicking and selecting Continue

3 Select the regions you want to keep



To change the selected regions, right-click, select Reset Current Selection and select other regions.

4 Click or to confirm your selections and cut the surfaces.



Click to discard your changes.
