Splitting a surface along U and V contemporarily

After starting the Split Surface command, load file "UVSplit.e3" and proceed as follows.

1 In the Type drop-down list, select UV Parameter, to split the surface contemporarily along an U and a V isoparametric curves.

2 Select the surface.
3 A handle and two mini-dialog boxes are displayed:



You can select the isoparametric curves to be used as cutting lines either by explicitly specifying the U and V values or by dragging the handle. A preview is immediately displayed.

4 Click or to confirm your selections and split the surface.



Click to discard your changes.
