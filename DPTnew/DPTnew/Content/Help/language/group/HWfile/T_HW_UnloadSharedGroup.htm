Unloading an X-Reference (groups)

After starting the Unload Shared Group command, proceed as follows.

The Unload Shared Group command can also be started by right-clicking on a shared group in the drawing or on its icon in the Drawing Structure and selecting Unload in the context menu.

1 Select the X-Reference group to be unloaded.



To change the selected group, right-click on Group, select Reset in the context menu and select another X-Reference group.

2 Click or to confirm and unload the X-Reference group. Any instances of the X-Reference group are not displayed any longer in the drawing, but their icons still appear in the Drawing Structure, updated by a red symbol representing the delete operation.



Click to discard your changes.
