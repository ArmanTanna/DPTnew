Edit

Undo Enables you to undo or reverse the previous action or actions
Redo Enables you to redo or restore the last action or actions previously undone
Cut Enables you to cut the selected element and store it to the clipboard
Copy Enables you to copy the selected element and store it to the clipboard
Paste Enables you to paste the object stored in the clipboard into the current document
Paste as Static Enables you to paste/import the stored object from the clipboard into the current or another document in a static (without any history events) way
Dimension Enables you to modify driving dimensions
Trim/Extend Curves Trims or extends both or the first of two selected curves
Trim/Extend Curves with Limits Trims the portion of selected entities that extend beyond two specified limit points or entities, or extends selected entities to the limit points or entities
Delete Enables you to delete entities
Smart Delete Enables you to delete part of a selected entity up to its nearest intersection with another entity
Rebuild Model Enables you to rebuild the model after changing the values of some of its parameters
Rebuild Step by Step Enables you to update the model one feature at a time after changing the values of some of its parameters
Regenerate Drawing Enables you to rebuild all the views of the current drawing (command available only when the drawing has not yet been updated after changes have been made to the referenced model)
Break Enables you to break selected entities into their component entities.
