Design Aids

Perpendicular Displays and creates the common perpendicular line to two normal or tangent vectors of curves/surfaces.
Tangent Plane Displays and creates the common tangent plane to two curve(s) and/or surface(s).
Mean Plane Displays and creates the mean plane considering the mean location of user selected entities, which can be points, curves or meshes.
Intersection on Extensions Creates the intersection line of two planar surfaces even when they don't actually intersect but their extensions do.
