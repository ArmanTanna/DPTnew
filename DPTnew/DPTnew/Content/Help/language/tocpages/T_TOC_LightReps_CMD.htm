Light Representations

Full Detail Applies a full-detailed representation to a/different X-Reference component(s), currently displayed in Light Representation
Flat Tessellation Applies a Light Representation at the lowest, quickest to load tessellation level to a/different X-Reference component(s)
Fast Tessellation Applies a Light Representation at an intermediate tessellation level to a/different X-Reference component(s)
Full Tessellation Applies a Light Representation at the highest tessellation level to a/different X-Reference component(s)
New Enables you to define a user provided Light Representation by selecting a model to be used in place of the X-Reference component as a Light Representation
Organize Enables you to arrange the different Light Representations for an X-Reference component
