Break Lines

Multibreaks on View Enables you to define a partial or broken view consisting of two separate portions of an existing view.
Half Break on View/Section Enables you to define a partial or broken view consisting of half of an existing view.
Insert Partial Break on View/Section Enables you to define a partial or broken view consisting of user-defined portion(s) of an existing view bounded by spline, rectangular, or circular border(s).
Modify Partial Break on View/Section Enables you to edit a partial or broken view defined by the Partial Break on View/Section command.
