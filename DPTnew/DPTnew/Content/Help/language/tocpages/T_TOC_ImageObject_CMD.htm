Image

Image Enables you to import an image.
Remove Enables you to remove an image.
Edit Enables you to translate, rotate and resize an image.
Move to Work Plane Enables you to move an image to the Work Plane once you have moved it.
Scaling Enables you to rescale an image.
Rotate Enables you to rotate an image.
Vertical Mirror Enables you to mirror an image about its own horizontal direction.
Horizontal Mirror Enables you to mirror an image about its own vertical direction.
Invert Colors Enables you to reverse the colors of an image.
Reposition on 2 Points Enables you to position an image by making two of its points coincide with two points of the model.
Hide Enables you to hide an image.
Unhide Enables you to unhide an image.
Hide All Enables you to hide all images.
Unhide All Enables you to unhide all images.
Info Enables you to view the details of an image.
