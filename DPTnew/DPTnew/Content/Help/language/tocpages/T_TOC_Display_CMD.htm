Display

Wireframe View Displays the objects in wireframe mode.
Boundary View Displays the objects in the model such that only the visible boundaries appear in the view.
Boundary View and Hidden Lines Displays the objects in the model such that the visible boundaries appear in the view as continuous lines. Surface boundaries that are hidden from view by other surfaces in the model (hidden lines) are also shown. Hidden lines are normally displayed as dashed lines.
Shaded View Displays the objects in the model as a colored, shaded rendering. No surface boundaries are shown.
Shaded View and Boundaries Displays the objects in the model as a colored, shaded rendering. Visible surface boundaries are also shown. Surface boundaries that are hidden from view by other surfaces in the model (hidden lines) are not displayed.
Shaded View Boundaries and Hidden Lines Displays the objects in the model as a colored, shaded rendering. Visible surface boundaries are shown as continuous lines. Surface boundaries that are hidden from view by other surfaces in the model (hidden lines) are also shown. Hidden lines are normally shown as dashed lines.
High Quality Shaded View Displays the objects in the model as a colored, shaded rendering and enables to use high quality graphics, which effects advanced options such as environmental mapping, reflections and shadows. No surface boundaries are shown.
High Quality Shaded View and Boundaries Displays the objects in the model as a colored, shaded rendering and enables to use high quality graphics, which effects advanced options such as environmental mapping, reflections and shadows. Visible surface boundaries are also shown. Surface boundaries that are hidden from view by other surfaces in the model (hidden lines) are not displayed.
High Quality Shaded View Boundaries and Hidden Lines Displays the objects in the model as a colored, shaded rendering and enables to use high quality graphics, which effects advanced options such as environmental mapping, reflections and shadows. Visible surface boundaries are shown as continuous lines. Surface boundaries that are hidden from view by other surfaces in the model (hidden lines) are also shown. Hidden lines are normally shown as dashed lines.
Viewports Displays the model in various viewports on the screen. You can choose to have the model displayed in one, two, three, or four viewports.
