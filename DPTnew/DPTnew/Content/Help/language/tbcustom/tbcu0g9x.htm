Copying and Pasting a Button

You can copy a button from one toolbar and paste it to another.

To copy a button, do the following:

  1. Select ToolsCustomize from the menu bar to open the Customize dialog box.
  2. Move the mouse pointer onto the button you want to copy.
  3. Right-click and choose Copy from the toolbar popup.

To paste the button you have just copied, do the following:

  1. Move the mouse pointer onto the toolbar you want to paste the button to.
  2. Right-click and choose Paste from the toolbar popup.
