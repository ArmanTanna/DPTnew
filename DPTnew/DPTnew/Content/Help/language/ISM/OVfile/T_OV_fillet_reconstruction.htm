Fillet Reconstruction in Interactive Solid Modeling

The Fillet Reconstruction option in the Move Faces and Offset Faces commands enable you to reconstruct the fillets when you move or offset the selected solids as shown in the image below.

The Fillet Reconstruction drop-down list consists of the following three options.

  • None: The feature will be moved without the fillet a shown in the image below:



  • Only Safe: When the feature is moved, then the fillet will be reconstructed based on the gradient of the face as shown in the image below.



  • All: When the feature is moved, then the whole fillet will be reconstructed irrespective of the gradient of the face as shown in the image below.



The fillet is reconstructed by evaluating the tolerance value entered in the Fillet Recognition Tolerance text box.
