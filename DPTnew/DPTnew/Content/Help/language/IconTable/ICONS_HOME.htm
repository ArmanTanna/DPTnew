Toolbar Icons: Old Look/New Look Map

The following map enables you to compare the Old and the New Look of command button icons of those categories — among the ones listed in the Commands tab of the Customize dialog box — for which the New toolbar icons differ from the Old ones, so as to help you easily recognize them.
As for the New Look, two different Sizes are displayed —  Small and Large — as the icons may differ in the two cases.
For details on how to change Look and Size, see "Choosing look and size for toolbar buttons".
