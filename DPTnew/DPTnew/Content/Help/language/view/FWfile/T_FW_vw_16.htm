The Pan/Zoom/Rotate command enables you to pan, zoom, or rotate or zoom the view of the modeldrawing by clicking the appropriate button on your mouse:

Button Function
Left pan
Center zoom
Right rotatepan

   

To dynamically zoom in/out, you can also use the mouse wheel (if any):

  • Rotate towards yourself to enlarge the image (zoom in)
  • Rotate away form yourself to reduce the image (zoom out).

See also "Panning, zooming and rotating and zooming".
