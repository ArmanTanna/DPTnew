ViewDisplayHigh Quality Shaded + Boundaries + Hidden Line


 

The High Quality Shaded View Boundaries and Hidden Lines command displays the objects in the model as a colored, shaded rendering and enables to use high quality graphics, which effects advanced options of the Environment, Lighting and Texture commands such as environmental mapping, reflections and shadows.
Visible surface boundaries are shown as continuous lines. Surface boundaries that are hidden from view by other surfaces in the model (hidden lines) are also shown. Hidden lines are normally shown as dashed lines. The line type for the hidden lines should be changed to "dashed" by selection the Dashed hidden line mode check box in the Graphics category in the System Options tab of Options/Properties dialog box.

Note
As all the visualization commands/features are really GPU intensive, user will require a High End and Professional Graphics Cards with updated display drivers to view them properly. You can verify your graphics card by clicking the Self Tune button in Graphics - Graphics Card in the System Options tab of Options/Properties dialog box.
