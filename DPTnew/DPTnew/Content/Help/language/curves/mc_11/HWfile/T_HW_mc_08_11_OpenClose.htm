Creating an open or a closed curve

When using the Curve through Interpolation Points command you can create a closed curve just by right-clicking and selecting Close Curve in th context menu. On the contrary, you can open the curve again by right-clicking and selecting Open Curve.

1 Suppose you creating an interpolation curve. If you want to close it, just right-click and select Close Curve



The result you will get will be a closed curve:
2 Now suppose you change your mind and prefer to keep the curve open. Right-click and select Open Curve
