The Use base surface check box is displayed when all of the selected chain of curves (except the planar ones, if any) are composed of curves of the Curve On Surface (COS) type.



  • When the check box is selected, the base surface of the COS curves is used to map the offset, thus obtaining the final curves.
  • When the check box is cleared, the base surface of the COS surface is not taken into account in the computation and the resulting curves are obtained according to the ordinary offset mechanism.
