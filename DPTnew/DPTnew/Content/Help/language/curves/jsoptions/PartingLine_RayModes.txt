PartingLine_RayModes="Enables you to select the type of light contour curve to be created:<ul><li><b>Silhouette</b> - To create silhouette curves which reproduce the silhouette of a surface relative to a chosen sight direction thus obtaining parting lines that are very useful when creating molds.</li><li><b>Highlights</b> - To create highlight curves (highlights are the loci of the points at which the light direction is perpendicular to the surface normal).</li><li><b>Isophotes</b> - To create isophote curves (isophotes are lines of constant illumination).</li></ul>"
