Bumps

Bumps is the indentations and unevenness provided on the surface. Using the bumps property, a smooth surface can be given an irregular and rough appearance.

The following are the Bumps options available in the drop down list:

None The surface is smooth without bumps.
Casting The surface will have an irregular casting pattern.
Rough The surface will have a rough, cast metal-like finish.
Wrapped bump map The surface can be mapped by a bump image.
Wrapped dimple The surface will have a regular dimpled appearance.
Wrapped knurl A knurled pattern will be visible on the wrapped image on the surface. The knurling pattern is similar to the grips seen on tool handles.
Wrapped rough The surface is wrapped with a rough, cast metal-like finish.
Wrapped tread plane The surface is wrapped with a regular tread plate pattern.
Bum wrapped leather Similar to the details of the surface of the leather.
Leather The surface will appear similar to leather.
