Wrapped Brick

A wrapped texture giving a simple brick pattern. All of the bricks have a uniform color which may be specified by Brick Color, and the color of the mortar may be specified by Mortar Color.

The width and height of each brick is specified by the settings Brick Width and Brick Height respectively, and the size of the mortar between the bricks (which is uniform between rows and columns of bricks) is specified by Mortar Size.
An overall scaling factor is provided with Scale; increasing this value will make the bricks appear larger.
