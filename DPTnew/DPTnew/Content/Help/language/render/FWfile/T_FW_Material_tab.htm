Material Tab

The Material tab is available in the Rendering Library palette. The Material tab displays the material library with predefined set of standard materials. All the materials are categorized and saved in an appropriate folder. By exploring the folders you can browse through the various materials in it and see their preview in the preview pane

  • Applying a Material
  • Edit a Material

Applying a Material

In the material tab, different materials are available in the predefined folders. The preview of the materials is seen in the preview pane of the material tab.
The following are the four method of applying a material.

  • Select the entity from the Graphics Area, right-click on the material in the preview pane and select Attach from the context menu.
  • Drag the material from the preview pane and drop it over the entity.
  • Right-click on the material in the preview pane, choose Attach from the context menu and select the entity in the Graphics Area. Then right click and select Cancel to end the selection.
  • Select the entity from the Graphics Area and double click on the material in the preview pane.
Once a material is attached, it is displayed in the Rendering structure under the Materials node.

Edit a Material

The applied material can be edited to your requirement using the Edit Material selection list. While editing the material, you can customize the visual properties like Texture Color, Reflectance, Transparency and Bumps. The Edit Material list can be started in two ways:

  • Right-click on the material name under the material node in the Rendering structure and select Edit from the context menu.
  • Right-click on the material surface on the model and select MaterialEdit from the context menu.
The following Visual Properties can be edited on the material applied.
Texture Color Enables you to edit the color and texture of the material applied.
Reflectance Enables you to edit the reflectance which is the amount of incident light being reflected from the material surface.
Transparency Enables you to edit the transparency which is the amount of light transmitted throught the material surface.
Bumps Enables you to edit the smoothness of the material surface.

Texture Mapping: Mapping of the textures can aslo be done if a wrapped material is applied. This option is available only if a wrapped material is applied.

Density: The density of the material applied can be defined under the physical properties. The unit of the density is set gm/cm³
