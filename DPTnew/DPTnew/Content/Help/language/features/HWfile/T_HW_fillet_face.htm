Creating Fillet on Selected Face

The Face Fillet command enables you to create a constant radius fillet on all the edges of a selected face of a solid. Open the Edge_Fillet_Simple.e3 file available in the Samples folder of your think3 installation and follow the steps given below.

  1. Star the Face Fillet command, you are prompted to select a face to fillet. Click on the face, as shown in the image below.



  2. Enter 5 in the Radius mini-dialog box to specify the fillet radius.

  3. Click on More Options to expand it and select the Continuous tangency check box from the Use Capping drop-down list.

  4. Click or to confirm your selections and fillet the edges of the face, as shown in the image below.
