There are about 6.900 Cadlabians (AKA GBGDM users) around. This is surprise, since at least 20 years DPT has stopped developing it! Nevertheless, there is still a lot of requests of new licenses. Moreover, some marketing or technical news about GBGDM introduce good arguments for a new campaign.


On the technical side, GBGDM v.9.1A7 is available on standard (and modern) USB dongles. Besides, it can also works with a node-locked password. Both implementations allow the Cadlabian to use GBGDM on modern PCs and modern operating systems (in fact, even if it has not been released for Win7 and Win8, it works properly on those systems as well). One can now open files with AutoCAD2000 format. GBGDM 9.1 allows to exceed the limit of 128.000 entities present in previous versions.


On the marketing side, GBGDM is now a bundle containing - on demand - all its existing add-ons.
