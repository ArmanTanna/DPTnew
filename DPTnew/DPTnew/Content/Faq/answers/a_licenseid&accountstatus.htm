Here are described the several kinds of Account Status available in DPT3Care.


Account Status Description
01 - Prospect Potential customers
02 - Dead Customer Dead companies
02 - Dead Partner Dead partners
03 - Active Customer Customers with some licenses under maintenance
03 - Active GBG Customer Customers that only have GBG licenses under maintenance
03 - Active Partner Active resellers and OEM partners
03 - Premium Customer 2018 Customers with all licenses (v.2015 and successive) under maintenance
03 - Premium Customer 2014 Customers with all licenses under maintenance (some or all v.2014 and prior)
04 - Not Active Customer Companies without a maintenance contract
04 - Not Active OM Customer Companies without a maintenance contract, they have only OPEN MIND products
04 - Not Active GBG Customer Companies that only have GBG products
05 - Ex Customer Companies that have had only ASF licenses, which have expired
06 - Blocked Customer Companies that have been blocked due to insolvency, etc.
