Your PC or your USB dongle is broken, or you have lost it or it got stolen? You cannot use your license for one of these reasons? Do not worry: write to DPT a letter, using one of the following templates, asking to enable again the use of the license.


ACME LTD

Dear DPT,

we hereby declare that we have updated the operating system of the workstation whose machine ID is XXXXXXXX. The upgrade has been done by reformatting the above mentioned workstation.

Therefore, we kindly request to DPT to issue a new license on such workstation/on a new workstation, according to its policy and modalities. We understand that this will result in the conversion of our permanent license (PL) into an annual temporary license (ASP).

We are aware that such self-certification form can be shared with those organizations that, by order of DPT, certify the presence of software applications’ licenses in the companies.

Kind regards


CEO Signature &

Warning 1: if the license involved in the breakdown, loss or theft is 'out of maintenance', DPT has the full right NOT to provide you with a new license. Do not get confused by the term permanent license: as you can read in the License Agreement (What about the License Agreement?) a permanent license gives you the right to use the product for an unlimited period of time exclusively on the computer officially and originally licensed by DPT.

By the way, what would happen if someone stole my Panda or if I crashed my Skoda? I'd have to buy a new car, in both cases.

You still have two options:

  • come back to maintenance
  • buy a CHW and then proceed with the request.

Warning 2: Permanent Licenses’ recovering after hardware crash or other events implicating license loss is NOT available. Therefore, if a Permanent License gets somehow lost or becomes irrecoverable as a result of the customer's actions, DPT can provide you for free just with a substitute license related to the maintenance contract .
This "new" ASP license will be active and working only until the end of the contract and will be renewed when the maintenance contract itself is renewed.
