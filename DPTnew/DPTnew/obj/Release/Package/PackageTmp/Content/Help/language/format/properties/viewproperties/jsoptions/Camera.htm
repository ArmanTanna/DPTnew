Enables you to place the camera at a selected point and to set the view direction along the invisible line between the camera and target position.
You can use temporary construction lines to help you position the camera and target points. For example, in the following illustration, the camera is positioned at the point indicated by the sphere (a). The view direction is indicated by the red line. The target is located at the right-hand endpoint of the red line (b). The resulting view is immediately reoriented based on the new view parameters.


See details about projection methods in "Orthographic and perspective views: general concepts".
