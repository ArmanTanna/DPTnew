Current

Current:

Enables you to set the current style by selecting it in the list of all the available ones.

Ordinate dimension

Ordinate dimension

The controls in this area enable you to determine the appearance for the Ordinate dimension.

Show dimension line

Show dimension line

Enables you to decide whether to show dimension line in Ordinate dimension or not.
When selected, the Ordinate dimension line is visible.
When not selected, the dimension line is not shown.

Show arrowhead

Show arrowhead

Enables you to decide whether to Show arrowhead in Ordinate dimension or not.
When selected, the Ordinate dimension includes an arrowhead.
When not selected, the arrowhead is not shown.

Diameter

Diameter

The controls in this area enable you to determine the format for Diameter dimensions.

As external label

As external label

Enables you to determine whether the Diameter dimensions should appear As external label or not.
When selected, Diameter dimensions appear as only an external label outside an arc or a circle.
When not selected, Diameter dimensions can include a dimension line and arrows at either end of the dimension.

Through center

Through center

Enables you to determine whether the Diameter dimensions should appear Through center or not.
When selected the Diameter dimension is created through the center of the arc or circle. This selection is not available when the As external label option check box is selected.
When not selected the Diameter dimension can be created otherwise.

Radius

Radius

The controls in this area enable you to determine the format for Radius dimensions.

Horizontal text position

Horizontal text position

Enables you to determine Horizontal text position for the Radius dimensions. You can choose one of the following options from the drop-down list:

Centered inside Enables you to position the text centerd inside the arc/circle.
Inside arc Enables you to position the text inside the arc/circle.
Outside arc Enables you to position the text outside the arc/circle.
Follow cursor Enables you to position the text based on the cursor position.
Free Enables you to position the text in any position.

Show center

Show center

Enables you to decide whether to Show center for an arc or a circle.
When selected a line is drawn from the center of the arc or circle up to the arrowhead of the dimension line.
When not selected the center of the above entity is not shown.

Arc length

Arc length

The controls in this area enables you to determine the appearance of the line and arrows of the Arc length dimensions.

Witness lines

Witness lines:

Enables you to specify the display of witness lines in the arc length dimension. Select one of the desired options from the drop-down list of Witness lines option.

Automatic Enables to draw the parallel witness lines when the sector angle greater than or equal to 180 degrees.
Radial Enables to draw the witness lines radially always.

Leader line

Leader line:

Enables you to specify the display of leader line in the arc length dimension. Select one of the desired options from the drop-down list of Leader line option.

Automatic Enables to place the leader line automatically when the arc sector angle is equal to or greater than 180 degrees.
Enabled Enables to place the leader line.
Disabled Enables to disable the placement of the leader line.

Leader line tail

Leader line tail:

Enables you to specify the display of leader line tail in the arc length dimension. Select one of the desired options from the drop-down list of Leader line tail option.

From text The tail of the leader line theoretically starts from the middle of the text and is visualized as soon as not overlapped by the text box.
From arc mid point The tail of the leader line is set to the mid point of the arc and the above said leader arrow.
