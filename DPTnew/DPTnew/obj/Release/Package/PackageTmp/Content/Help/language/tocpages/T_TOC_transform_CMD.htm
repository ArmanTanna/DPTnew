Geometric Transformations

Mirror Entities Enables you to quickly mirror entities about a symmetry plane (in the 3D environment) or about a symmetry axis (in the 2D environment).
Move Copy Entities Enables you to perform translations, rotations and rototranslations.
Multistretch Entities Stretches those entities crossing a rectangular selection window and translates those entities enclosed within the window.
Scale Entities Enables you to homogeneously scale selected entities, or to perform mono-directional scaling (stretching) along one direction.
