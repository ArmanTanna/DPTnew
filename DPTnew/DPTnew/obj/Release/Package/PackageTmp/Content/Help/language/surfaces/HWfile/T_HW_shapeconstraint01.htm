Moving the selected location without modifying the tangency conditions

By properly selecting the handle, you can move the location on the surface thus modifying its shape maintaining its tangency conditions unchanged.

1 Select the surface by clicking on the location you want to edit. A cross handle will be displayed in such location.
2 Click again on the selected location, which is the center point of the cross handle and has a marker on it.
3 Drag it to the new position and release the mouse button. The shape of the surface will be modified but its tangency conditions in the selected location remain unchanged.
