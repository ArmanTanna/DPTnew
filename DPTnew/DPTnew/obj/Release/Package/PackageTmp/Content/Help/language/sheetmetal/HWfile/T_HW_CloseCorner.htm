Closing the corner between two adjacent walls

1 After starting the Close Corner command, select the two side faces of the involved walls.



To change the selected faces, right-click on Face, choose Reset in the context menu and perform selection again.
2 In the Type drop-down list, select the type of closure to be performed (note that the closure will be performed taking into account the Gap value defined in the Rip/Corner page inside Sheet Metal category of the Entity Properties). To change it locally, proceed as described in Local Properties and the Lock Marker).
 
Symmetric the two side walls are extended symmetrically along the bisector line of the corner angle formed by the faces.
Full the first wall is extended up to its intersection with the projection of the second. To extend the second wall rather than the first, click the red arrow displayed on the face.
Middle the first wall is extended up to the middle line of the second. To extend the second wall rather than the first, click the red arrow displayed on the face.
3 Click or to confirm your selections and close the corner.
