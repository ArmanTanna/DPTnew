Solid Clouds

A solid texture giving a cloudy appearance. The color of the clouds and the background (sky) may be specified in settings Clouds Color and BackGround Color respectively.

The detail or complexity of the texture may be controlled by means of the Detail value. A value of 1 will give a simple outline of the clouds, while a larger value such as 5 or 6 will give fine detail on the cloud outlines.
An overall scaling factor is provided with Scale; increasing this value will make the clouds appear larger.
