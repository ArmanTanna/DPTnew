Splitting a surface along an isoparametric curve through an arc joint

After starting the Split Surface command, proceed as follows.

1 In the Type drop-down list, select the Arc Joints method.

2 Select the surface.



to change the selected surface, right-click on Surface, choose Reset in the context menu and select another surface.

3 The arc joints of the surface along the two parametric directions are displayed. Click on one of the arc joints:



A preview of the isoparametric curve is immediately displayed.



To change the selected arc joint, right-click on Point, choose Reset in the context menu and select another one.

4 Click or to confirm your selections and split the surface.



Click to discard your changes.
