The controls in this group enable you to specify the user margins of the area you want to print relative to the sheet you are using.
In some printers, the printing area does not cover the full size of the sheet but is contained inside the sheet. Therefore, if the margins set do not take into account the area that can be printed by the printer, the resulting print may be incomplete.



To avoid problems of this kind, remember that the margins must be wide enough to keep the size of the print within the printing area of the printer.
