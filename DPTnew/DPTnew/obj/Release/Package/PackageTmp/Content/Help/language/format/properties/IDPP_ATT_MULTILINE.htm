Entity Properties

Category: Text-Align

Current

Current:

Enables you to set the current style by selecting it in the list of all the available ones.

Standard

Standard

The controls in this area enable you to choose a proper alignment for the text characters.

Align

Align:

Enables you to choose the justification for single line texts. Single line texts are to be considered contained in a grid made of four horizontal lines and three vertical lines, as displayed in the following illustration:



where:

  • h1  is the Bottom line
  • h2  is the Base line
  • h3  is the Middle line
  • h4  is the Top line
  • v1  is the Left line
  • v2  is the Center line
  • v3  is the Right line

and points from 1 to 12 are the intersections of the said lines, where the text origin will be placed if you choose the corresponding option:

  • 1 — Left
  • 2 — Center
  • 3 — Right
  • 4 — Middle left
  • 5 — Middle center
  • 5' — Middle
  • 6 — Middle right
  • 7 — Top left
  • 8 — Top center
  • 9 — Top right
  • 10 — Bottom left
  • 11 — Bottom center
  • 12 — Bottom right

Note
  • Middle is the center of the actual text bounding box.
  • Middle and Middle center will coincide, if the text string contains no descending letters such as "p" or "q" or "g" etc.

Angle

Angle:

Enables you to specify the Angle at which the text is positioned. The angle you specify here is measured relative to the x-axis and in counterclockwise direction. The default angle is 0.

Multiline

Multiline

The controls in this area enable you to determine various options for the Multiline text.

Align

Align:

Enables you to choose the alignment for multiline texts. The bounding box of multiline texts is be considered contained in a grid made of three horizontal lines and three vertical lines, as displayed in the following illustration:

  • h1  is the Bottom line

  • 9 — Top right

Line spacing

Line spacing:

Enables you to specify the Line spacing for multiline text, as a percentage of the height of the characters.

Wrap text

Wrap text

Enables you to choose the Wrap text option for the Multiline text by clicking on the check box, provided in this section.

Enable

Enable

Enables you to control multiline text wrapping.

  • When selected, multiline texts are wrapped around the corner according to the width of each bounding box and/or to the current Width value.
  • When not selected, multiline texts are not wrapped.

Width

Width:

Enables you to specify the Width of each text line. If the text line exceeds this value, then the text will wrap.
