Transparency

Transparency is the physical property of allowing light to pass through the material. The intensity of the transparency is measured by the amount of light that is transmitted through the surface.

The following are the Transparency options available in the drop down list:

None The surface will resemble like an opaque surface.
Plain A uniform transparency on the surface.
Eroded The transparent surface has the illusion of erosion.
Wrapped checker The transparent surface has a wrapped checker board pattern.
Wrapped grid A transparent surface with grid pattern.
Wrapped image A transparent surface with an option to wrap the image on the transparent surface.
Wrapped mask A transparent surface wrapped with an image. The transparency is channeled in a particular selected color.
Wrapped square A square region of the surface is made transparent.
Glow Creates an effect of glow around the transparent surface.
Plain coverage A uniform transparency is visualized in the surface.

Note
All the options in the selection list which are in Bold or appended with (*) sign are used for static rendering, that is the changes made in these parameters are visible only in static rendering.
