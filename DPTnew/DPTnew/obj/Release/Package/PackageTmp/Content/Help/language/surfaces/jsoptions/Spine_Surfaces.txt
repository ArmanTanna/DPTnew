Spine_Surfaces="Enables you to define how to build the resulting surface(s), when connecting more than two boundaries or whenthere is just one boundary byt the spine is made of a chain of curves: One surface -- the result will be just one surface connecting all the boundaries and independently from the how many curves the spine curve is made of; Many surfaces -- the result will be as many surfaces as the ones needed to connect each couple of subsequent boundaries/curves belonging to the spine"
