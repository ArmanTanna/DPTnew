You can create rectangles and polygons using the following commands:
 

  • Rectangle
    enables you to create a rectangle:
    • based on two opposite vertices (corners)
    • its center, the length of its sides, and its orientation angle
       
  • Polygon
    enables you to create an inscribed or circumscribed polygon based on its radius, number of sides, and orientation angle.

When working in the model (three-dimensional) environment, the resulting entities are created on the Work Plane.
