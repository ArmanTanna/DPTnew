Blue Marble

A solid texture giving a blue marble appearance.

The detail or complexity of the texture may be controlled by the Detail value. A value of 1 will give a simple outline of the marble pattern, while a larger value such as 5 or 6 will give fine detail in the texture.

An overall scaling factor is provided with Scale edit box; increasing this value will make the marble texture appear larger.
