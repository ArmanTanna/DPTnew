Control the surface continuity

You can modify the magnitude of tangent vectors while keeping their direction unchanged. To do so:

1 Select the surface by clicking on the location you want to edit.
A cross handle will be displayed in such location.
2 Select the type of continuity you want to apply from the Snap drop-down list:
  • Positional — positional continuity
  • Tangent — tangency continuity
  • Perpendicular — perpendicularity continuity
3 Drag the selected location of the surface and snap it to the other surface you want to apply the continuity conditions to. The continuity type you specified will be applied.
