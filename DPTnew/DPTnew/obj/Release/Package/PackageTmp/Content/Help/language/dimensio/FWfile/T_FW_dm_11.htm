ModifyDimensionMove

The Move Dimension command enables you to move portions of a dimension.

Also, double-clicking on a dimension will start the Move Dimension command. This will facilitate the movement of the measure line or text and the change in length for the witness line.

  • Modify individual portions of the dimension
