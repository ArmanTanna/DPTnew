PARTsolutions plug-in removal

To remove PARTsolutions plug-in from your system, follow these steps:

  1. Click StartControl Panel.

  2. Double-click Add or Remove Programs.

  3. Locate PARTSolutions XXX (where XXX represents the installed version of the plug-in) and click to select it.

  4. Click Change/Remove, confirm the removal of the software and wait for the confirmation message.
