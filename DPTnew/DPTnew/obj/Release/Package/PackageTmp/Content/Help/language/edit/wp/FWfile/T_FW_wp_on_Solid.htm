Work Plane Here command

The Work Plane Here command allows you to place the Work Plane on the planer face or datum plane. To do so, right-click on the face and select Work Plane Here from the context menu. The Work Plane placed on the planer face is shown in the images below.



Alternatively, you can place the Work Plane by double-clicking on the planer face or datum plane.
