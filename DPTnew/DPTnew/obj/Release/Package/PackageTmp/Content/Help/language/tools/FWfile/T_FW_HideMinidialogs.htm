The Hide Minidialogs command enables you to temporarily conceal the visualization of the minidialogs.

You can find this command in the context menu that becomes available when you right-click in the graphic area whenever the minidialogs are visible, or you can run the command by hitting the F7 key.

From time to time, the presence of minidialogs on the screen may prevent you from correctly evaluating the effect of the command preview; in this case, concealing the minidialogs may help you take the right decision faster.

The Hide Minidialogs command is automatically reset when you apply or cancel the feature.
