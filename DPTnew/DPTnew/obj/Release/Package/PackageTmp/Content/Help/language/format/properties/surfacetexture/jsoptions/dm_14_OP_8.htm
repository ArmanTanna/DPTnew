Enables you to specify the type of lay direction to be used, by selecting one of the following from the drop-down list:

None Enables you to specify no lay direction.
Parallel Enables you to lay in the direction parallel to the line representing the surface to which the surface texture symbol is applied.
Perpendicular Enables you to lay in the direction perpendicular to the line representing the surface to which the surface texture symbol is applied.
Intersect Enables you to lay angular in both directions to the line representing the surface to which the surface texture symbol is applied.
Multidirection Enables you to lay in any direction to the line representing the surface to which the surface texture symbol is applied.
Circular Enables you to lay in circular direction relative to the center of the surface to which the surface texture symbol is applied.
Radial Enables you to lay in radial directions relative to the center of the surface to which the surface texture symbol is applied.
Non directional Enables you to lay non directional to the surface texture symbol is applied.
