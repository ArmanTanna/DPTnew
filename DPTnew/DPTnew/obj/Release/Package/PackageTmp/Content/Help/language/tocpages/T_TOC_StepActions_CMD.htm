Step Actions

Zoom in Enlarges the image in the active view
Zoom out Reduces the image in the active view
Pan right Shifts the image in the selected view to the right by one incremental step
Pan left Shifts the image in the selected view to the left by one incremental step
Pan down Shifts the image in the selected view down by one incremental step
Pan up Shifts the image in the selected view up by one incremental step

Rotate Counter-Clockwise about X Rotates the object in the active view counter-clockwise about the X-axis by one incremental step
Rotate Counter-Clockwise about Y Rotates the object in the active view counter-clockwise about the Y-axis by one incremental step
