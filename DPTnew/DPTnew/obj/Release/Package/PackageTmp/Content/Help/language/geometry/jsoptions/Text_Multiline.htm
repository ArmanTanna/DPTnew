Multiline Mode            Enables you to choose whether to insert single or multiline text.
  • When the box is cleared, you can type your single line text in the adjacent box.
  • When the Multiline Mode check box is selected, three dots are shown on the Text option; clicking on the dots a dialog box will show up so as to enable you to type in your multiline text. Press the Enter key of the keyboard to move on to next line. Click OK to actually add the supplied text to your document.
