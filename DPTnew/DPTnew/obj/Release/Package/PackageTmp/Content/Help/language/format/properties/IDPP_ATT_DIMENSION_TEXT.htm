Entity Properties

Category: Dimension-Text

Current

Current:

Enables you to set the current style by selecting it in the list of all the available ones.

Text appearance

Text appearance

The controls in this area enable you to determine the Text appearance for the dimension texts. The dimension texts could be Prefix, Suffix, etc.

Text size

Text size:

Enables you to specify the dimension Text size.

Text style

Text style:

In the Organize Styles dialog box, it enables you to determine the Text style for the dimension texts. All the Loaded styles are available in the drop-down list.
In the Entity Properties dialog box, it just enables you to view the current style. The drop-down list is greyed out to indicate that you cannot change the style here, except in Organize Styles dialog box.

Text orientation

Always horizontal

Enables you to control the orientation of the dimension text.

  • When selected, the dimension text is always displayed horizontally.
  • When not selected, the dimension text orientation matches that of the dimension line.

Always horizontal

Always horizontal

Enables you to control the orientation of the dimension text.

  • When selected, the dimension text is always displayed horizontally.
  • When not selected, the dimension text orientation matches that of the dimension line.

Text placement

Text placement

The controls in this area enable you to determine the dimension Text placement with respect to the dimension line.

Horizontal

Horizontal:

Enables you to determine the dimension Text placement with respect to the dimension line in the Horizontal position using one of the following options:

Free Enables you to place the dimension text anywhere on the dimension line.
Center Enables you to place the dimension text at the Center on the dimension line.
Left Enables you to place the dimension text to the Left of the dimension line.
Right Enables you to place the dimension text to the Right of the dimension line.
Follow cursor Enables you to place the dimension text depending on the position of the cursor.
Center Fixed Enables you to place the dimension text at the Fixed center on the dimension line.
Left inside Enables you to place the dimension text at the Left inside portion of the dimension line.
Right inside Enables you to place the dimension text at the Right inside portion of the dimension line.
Along left line Enables you to place the dimension text along the left extension line of the dimension line.
Along right line Enables you to place the dimension text along the right extension line of the dimension line.

Vertical

Vertical:

Enables you to determine the Vertical position of the dimension with respect to the dimension line using one of the following options:

Above Enables you to place the dimension text vertically Above the dimension line.
Middle Enables you to place the dimension text vertically at the Middle of the dimension line.
Below Enables you to place the dimension text vertically Below the dimension line.

Line spacing

Line spacing:

The controls in this area enable you to define various parameters for the Line spacing, as a ratio with respect to the dimension text size.

Horizontal

Horizontal:

Enables you to specify the the horizontal distance of the dimension text from the Extension line as a ratio with respect to the text size.

Vertical

Vertical:

Enables you to specify the Vertical distance of the dimension text from the Dimension line, as a ratio with respect to the text size.
