Enables you to choose the rectangular portion of the graphics area of the drawing/model you want to print:

  • Automatic — prints all the visible contents of the drawing/model.
  • Current — prints the contents of the active window on the drawing/model.
  • Custom — prints the rectangular portion of the drawing/model that you specify by typing the coordinates of two opposite corners of it in the boxes below.




  • Alternatively, click the (Select) button and click on the two opposite corners directly in the drawing/model. The coordinates are entered in the boxes automatically.
    These coordinates will be used for subsequent print operations until you change them again.
  • Ask each time — prints the rectangular portion of the drawing/model that you indicate by clicking on two opposite corners directly in the drawing/model as soon as you choose the Print command. When this option is selected, you must indicate two points in this way each time you print.
