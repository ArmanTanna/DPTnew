Changing the flexibility of a surface

The Surface Flexibility node in the selection list of the Surface through Control Points command enables you to change the flexibility of a selected surface in terms of degree and continuity.

You can also change the values of the Boundary continuity order ( Position, Tangent, Curvature), of the Max. deg. U (maximum degree along the U parametric direction) and Max. deg. V (maximum degree along the V parametric direction) and of the Max. arcs U (maximum number of arcs along the U parametric direction) and Max. arcs V (maximum number of arcs along the V parametric direction) as well as the Tolerance value to be used ( Position, Tangent, Curvature).



Any time you change one of the above said values, click Edit flexibility to apply it to the surface that is being created/edited and you will appreciate the change in the preview.



Once you have modified any of the above said data, if the preview of the result you would obtain does not satisfy you, you can reject it just by using the internal Undo () button.
