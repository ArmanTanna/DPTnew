When the intermittent bead segments are shorter than the bead length, you are enabled to align the welding segments according to one of the criteria in the drop-down list:

  • To the center
    An equal space is left between the starting point of the welding and the first segment and between the ending point of the welding and the last segment.
  • To the start
    The first segment starts at the starting point of the welding.
  • To the end
    The last segment ends at the ending point of the welding.
