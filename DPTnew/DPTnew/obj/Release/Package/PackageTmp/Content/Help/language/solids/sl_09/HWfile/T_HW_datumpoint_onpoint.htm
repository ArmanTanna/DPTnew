Inserting a datum point on a point

The Datum Point command enables you to insert a datum point on a vertex of the object.

1 In the Point on drop-down list select Vertex.
2 Click to select a vertex of a solid.



To change the selected vertex, right-click on Vertex in the selection list, choose Reset in the context menu and select another vertex.
3 Click or to confirm your selections and create the point. Click to discard your changes.
