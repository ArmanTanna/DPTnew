Creating Surfaces

Pipe Surface Creates circular section surfaces (pipes) along a path defined by a chain of drive curves, which can also contain sharp points.
Blending Shapes Creates blending surfaces connecting two chains of consecutive curves and/or surface boundaries controlling their smoothness and matching required continuity conditions along the selected contours.
Break Solid Breaks a solid into its component surfaces.
Capping Creates a capping surface through curve/points and along boundaries.
Connect Lofted Surface Creates a connection surface between two (or more) existing surfaces.
Connect Surface Creates a surface which connects two or more specified surfaces.
Double Fillet Creates a blend surface — either rounded or straight — between two selected surfaces.
Drafter Creates surfaces along a drive contour and tangent to some other surfaces used as drives.
Full Round Fillet Creates a fillet tangent to three different surfaces.
Global Sweep Creates surfaces by sweeping one or more contours — chain of consecutive curves along one or more drive contours —, even if they have sharp points.
Grid Lofted Surface Creates a surface from a mesh of curves formed by two almost orthogonal subsets of approximately parallel curves.
Helix Surface Creates a helical surface by sweeping existing curves along a helical path.
Join Surface Enables you to join two or more surfaces to form a single surface.
Linear Surface Creates surfaces by sweeping curves, a profile, or even edges of solids along a linear path.
Lofted Surface Creates "lofted" surfaces which are best suited to the input parameters.
Offset Surface Creates an offset surface
Fuzzy Offset Creates a fuzzy offset surface
Parting Plane Creates a parting plane: a surface located around the parting line (generally made of a chain of curves) that is very useful when creating molds.
Planar Development Creates curves for the planar development of a ruled surface.
Plane Surface Creates a plane
Point Cloud Creates surfaces which fit sets of points./Interpolate a set of points which has some regularity characteristics.
Proportional Lofted Surface Creates surfaces by taking one or two boundary curves along one or two drives, which may be curves or surfaces.
Rake Sweep Enables you to create a surface by sweeping one rigid contour along two drive contours< and fulfilling some contact constraints.
Rotational Surface Creates a surface by revolving a set of selected curves about an axis in any direction.
Rotational Sweep Enables you to create rotational sweep surfaces by revolving a set of sections about an axis you can specify directly or let the program use a default one.
Ruled Lofted Surface Creates a ruled surface.
Spined Surface Creates a surface by moving one or more boundary curves along one or two drive curves, and controlling its shape by means of an auxiliary curve, called a spine.
Spined Fillet Creates fillet surfaces between two groups of surfaces (the number of surfaces in the first group may differ from that in the second group) from a spine curve chain and two drive curve chains, if necessary.
Stretched Lofted Surface Creates a stretched surface from three or four boundaries.
Primitive on Mesh Creates primitive surfaces among planes and cylinders fitting mesh zones and fulfilling constraints.
