Clearance Terminologies

Clearance is the relief given to the inserted standard parts in the core and plates of the mold base. The clearance dialog box is pictorially represented and has to specify the values in the text boxs. The following are the terminologies:
Guide length The length of the pin where clearance is not provided.
Collar offset The clearance between the head and pin of the standard part.
Shoulder length The clearance for the shoulder part of the standard part
Head offset The diametrical clearance given around the head of the standard part.
Ejection delay The clearance given which will result in a delay in the movement of the standard part by a certain interval of time.
Top offset The clearance given to the top face of the standard part.
Bottom offset The clearance given to the bottom face of the standard part
