Creating a parabola through three points

After starting the Parabola command, proceed as follows.

1 Type drop-down list, select 3 points.

2 Specify the first endpoint of the parabolic arc, a through point, and the second endpoint by clicking or using Snap.



To change the selected points, right-click on Point, select Reset in the context menu and specify the new points.
3 Click or to confirm your selections and create the curve.



Click to discard your changes.
