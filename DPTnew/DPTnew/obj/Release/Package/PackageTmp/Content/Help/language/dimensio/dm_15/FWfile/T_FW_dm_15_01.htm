The Length Type of the Smart Dimension command creates a linear dimension for the length of an entity.

You can specify the Orientation as one of the following:

  • Distance — parallel to the line through the endpoints of the entity
  • Horizontal — parallel to the X-axis
  • Vertical — parallel to the Y-axis
  • Projected — projected at a specified angle
  • Automatic — parallel to the line through the endpoints of the entity or parallel to either the X-axis or the Y-axis
