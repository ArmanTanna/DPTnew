Parasolid

Category: Save

Convert profiles to curves

When selected, these entities are converted. When not selected they are ignored.

Convert datum entities

Controls the conversion of datum entities. When this check-box is selected, datum entities are converted.

Convert not visible entities

When selected, these entities are converted. When not selected they are ignored.

Convert hierarchy

When selected, if present, the model hierarchy is converted.

Convert solid entities

Controls the conversion of solid entities. When this check-box is not selected, solids are converted into their component surfaces.

Convert non-manifold solid

When selected, non manifold solids are converted. When not selected, only manifold solids are converted.

Split closed faces/surfaces

Some of the CAD applications cannot handle revolved surfaces. To overcome this problem the revolved surface can be split into its individual component surfaces that can then be managed by the CAD application. When the check box is selected, closed surfaces and solid faces are split into their component surfaces and when not selected, they are not split.

Show warnings/error messages

When selected, warnings and error messages are displayed in a popup window.
