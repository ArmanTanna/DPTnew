Overview about TTeam products.



During the years DPT has released more than 30 products of the 'TT Family'. Now there are just 7 products. A map of equivalences is required. Here it is.


Important Notice: 'equivalence' does not mean that products in the right column of the table below are equal to those in the left column of the same table. It means that a user of a Legacy Product will get 'for free' an Equivalent Product in order he is not missing any of the features he previouslygot. It means also that such user should now buy PLSS or ASF for the Equivalent Product.
