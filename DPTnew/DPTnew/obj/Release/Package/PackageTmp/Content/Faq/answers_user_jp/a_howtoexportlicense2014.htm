Warning: if you have the right to export your license (see previous FAQ), you can keep reading and export it. On the contrary, please stop now and do not export it, as you will not get one as replacement!

From the PC where the license is on, launch TD and select Help → License Administrator. Then apply the Edit → Export Licenses command.

You will get the following message, asking for your confirm to disable the license you are exporting.




[Please, note that you can launch cladimin.exe also by following this path:
C:\Program Files\think3\2014.1\common\cladmin.exe.]


The Export License Wizard opens, taking you through 4 steps:

  1. From the list of the exportable products, select the desired one(s). Click Next.
  2. Select the Manual option. Click Next.
  3. In the License file to be exported box, specify name and destination of the .tbu for its download. Alternatively, use the Browse button on the right to open the system window Save As, and specìfy the file. Click Next.
  4. The form shows the selected products, the name of the .tbu file and the export mode you have chosen (Manual). Confìrm to Finish the export of the license(s).

Now you can send an email to pwdrelease@dptcorporate.com, attaching the .tbu file and specifying your company name, your name and surname and your email address.


As soon as possible, you will get from DPT the password(s) for the new license(s). Insert it on the new PC using the License Administrator.
