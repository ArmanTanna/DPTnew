Three new modules have been added to our catalogue: TDBase, TDEngineeringPlus and TDMolding.


On the same day, eight modules have reached their end-of-life (EoL). They are:

  • 3 "F" modules: TDEngineeringF, TDToolingF and TDProfessionalF
  • 2 "S" modules: TDEngineeringS and TDToolingS
  • 3 "oem" modules: Gengine, ThinkOM and ThinkComputes


No 'EoL' module can be sold anymore, both as new licenses and as maintenance. However, each of them has a perfect replacement picking other products from the catalogue.


TDEngineeringF was introduced on April 2014 to smooth an ASF price increase, now absorbed.

replaced by comparison
TDEngineering same features, slightly higher price

TDToolingF was introduced on April 2014 to absorb a PLSS price increase.

replaced by comparison
TDEngineeringPlus same price, slightly lesser features
TDTooling same features, higher price

TDProfessionalF was introduced in April 2014 to smooth a PL & PLSS price increase, for user of MoldDesign or thinkID.

replaced by comparison
TDMolding same price, all features expected by moldmakers
TDProfessional complete set of features, higher price

TDEngineeringS was introduced in May 2011 to compete against low cost CAD application such as OpenMind's or Alma's.

replaced by comparison

TDToolingS was introduced in May 2011 to compete against low cost CAD application such as OpenMind's or Alma's.

replaced by comparison

Final note: by considering "F" & "S" modules EoL and by introducing TDBase, TDEngineeringPlus, TDMolding, DPT is finally adopting the WIPIWYG (What-You-Pay-Is-What-You-Get) policy. Clean and clear and more profitable for VARs in terms of recurrent maintenance.
