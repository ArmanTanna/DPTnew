Select the "Case Management" section in the bottom left corner of the home page.





A new tab will open. To open a Case, click the "New" button.




Fill out the fields with all required information. To upload an attachment, select the "Choose file" button.
ATTENTION: you can upload only a file whose size does not exceed 1 GB.
If you need to upload more documents, create a .zip file!





Select the "Submit" button.
If the operation is successful, the following message will appear:





Have you forgotten to write something or to insert the attachment?
Do not worry, you can update the Case!

Go back to "Case Management" section and reload the page (ctrl + F5).
The row corresponding to the Case just inserted will appear. After selecting it (do NOT click the green cross button, but the row itself), the "Update" button will be enabled, as shown in the picture below.





After selecting "Update", a new tab will open.
Fill in the fields you need to update and click then "Submit".





In order to view the details and the attachments of your Case, go back to "Case Management" section and reload the page (ctrl + F5).
Select the row corresponding to the Case just inserted (remember NOT to click the green cross button, but the row itself) and click the "History" button.
