You want to change your PC, whatever the reason, and your license is 'out of maintenance'? This is when you need to 'buy' a CHW or Change Hardware. This contract does not entitle you to receive new product versions or updates or Customer Care services. It simply allows you to get your license, in the same version, on a different PC. Your CHW order should come together with a letter based on the following template.


ACME LTD

Dear DPT,

we hereby declare that we have changed our workstation. The old machineID was XXXXXXXX, while the new machineID is YYYYYYYY.

We kindly request to DPT to enable the use of think3 license on the new workstation.

Kind regards


CEO Signature &
Company Stamp
