The use of DPT software is ruled by a License Agreement which is embedded into our solutions.


This is an abstract from the License Agreement, regarding some definitions:

Business Description
PL Permanent License: the Customer has the license to use the product for an unlimited time. According to the General Conditions (ยง6 Product Use) the Customer has the right to permanently use the product only on the computer officially and initially licensed by DPT.
PLSS Permanent License Service Subscription: during the period of validity of this subscription, the Customer is entitled to receive new product versions or updates together with Customer Care services. Besides, one (1) time per solar year, the Customer can export the license from the computer where it is officially licensed to another one.
ASF Annual subscription fee: the Customer has the right to use the license of the product for 12 months. When the license expires the product is no longer usable. During the period of validity of the ASF, the Customer is entitled to receive new product versions or updates from DPT, together with Customer Care services.
CVU Current Version Upgrade: 'back in maintenance' fee in order to reactivate an annual PLSS for a Permanent License.
CHW Change Hardware: fee to export once a license 'out of maintenance'. With this contract the Customer can export such license from the computer where it is officially licensed to another one. This contract does not entitle the Customer to receive new product versions or updates or Customer Care services.

Very important for the correct management of 'export of licenses' is what is provided in clause 6. Product Use. Here is an abstract:


The Customer has the right to use the Product and any Product Release only on the computer originally indicated to DPT..
(...)
The Customer with a PLSS subscription has the right to export each license covered by such a contract, only one (1) time in a calendar year, from the computer on which the license has been originally installed, to another computer of his/her property.
The Customer with a license to Use the Product (PL), without a PLSS subscription, who wants to exploit the services provided with such a subscription, can re-activate it by signing a Current Version Upgrade (CVU) contract.
The Customer with a license to Use the Product (PL), without a PLSS subscription, who needs to export his/her license from the computer on which it has been originally installed to another computer of his/her property, can subscribe a Change Hardware (CHW) contract. This contract does not provide any updates or new version releases, which are instead covered by the PLSS subscription.
