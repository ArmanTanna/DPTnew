TDXchangereader list price is 2000€, plus 500€ for maintenance. It is able to read CAD data of any version of:

  • SolidWorks© up to 2016
  • Autodesk® Inventor® up to 2017
  • JT version up to 10.0
  • Siemens NX (Unigraphics) from V11.0 up to NX 10.0
  • CATIA® V5 from R4 up to V5-6R2016
  • CATIA® V6 from 2011 up to 2013
  • Parasolid® up to v27.0
  • Pro/ENGINEER up to Wildfire 5
  • Creo Parametric up to 3.0

TDirectRW list price is 4500€, without maintenance. It is a 'marketing bundle' of TDirectCatiaRW, TDirectParasolidRW & TDirectProeRW, whose list prices amounted to 1200€, without maintenance
With TDirectRW, directly in TD environment it is possible to save files in the following formats:

  • CATIA® V5 up to rel.7 (.catp)
  • Parasolid® up to V.11 (.x_t)
  • Pro/ENGINEER up to Pro/E® 2000i2 (.neu)
