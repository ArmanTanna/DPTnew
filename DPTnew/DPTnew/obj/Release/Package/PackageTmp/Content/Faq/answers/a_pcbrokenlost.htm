Your PC or your USB dongle is broken, or you have lost it or it got stolen? You cannot use your license for one of these reasons? Do not worry: write to DPT a letter, using one of the following templates, asking to enable again the use of the license.


ACME LTD

Dear DPT,

we hereby declare that we have updated the operating system of the workstation whose machineID is XXXXXXXX. The upgrade has been done by reformatting the above mentioned workstation.

We kindly request to DPT to enable the use of think3 license on such workstation, whose machineID is now YYYYYYYY.

Kind regards


CEO Signature &
Company Stamp


or


ACME LTD

Dear DPT,

we hereby declare that we our workstation, whose machineID was XXXXXXXX, got lost/stolen. Please find in attachment the official notification of loss/theft.

We kindly request to DPT to enable the use of think3 license on a new workstation, whose machineID is YYYYYYYY.

Kind regards


CEO Signature &
Company Stamp



Warning: if the license involved in the breakdown, loss or theft is 'out of maintenance', DPT has the full right NOT to provide you with a new license. Do not get confused by the term permanent license: as you can read in the License Agreement (What about the License Agreement?) a permanent license gives the user the right to permanently use the product only on the computer officially and initially licensed by DPT.

By the way, remember what happened when they stole my Panda or when I crashed my Skoda: I was to buy a new car, in both cases.

You have stile two options:

  • buy a CVU so that the license will be 'back in maintenance'
  • buy a CHW and the proceed with the request.
