The use of DPT software is ruled by a License Agreement which is embedded into our solutions.


This is an abstract from the License Agreement, regarding some definitions:

Business Description
PL Permanent License (PL): under this License the Customer can use the Software for an unlimited period of time exclusively on the PC on which the Software was originally installed.
PLSS Permanent License Service Subscription (PLSS): is the annual subscription for a Permanent License (PL) under which Customer is entitled to receive the Updates together with the Software Support and Maintenance Services. Under this subscription agreement DPT will send to Customer the Software Updates, issued during the Subscription Period. Customer with a PLSS subscription for a Permanent License (PL) has the right to Export such License once (1 time) during the Subscription Period, from the computer on which such License was originally installed, to another computer of its own.
ASF Annual Subscription (ASF or ASP): under this subscription agreement, Customer has the right to use the License for 12 months. Upon expiry of the Subscription Period (as defined hereunder) the Software is no longer usable. The ASF or ASP subscription entitles Customer, during the Subscription Period, to the transmission by DPT of Updates and Software Support and Maintenance Services.
CVU Current Version Upgrade: means the contract under which Customer using a Permanent License (PL), without a PLSS subscription, who wish to take advantage of the services provided for in that subscription, reactivates the PLSS subscription and is therefore entitled to all Software Support and Maintenance Services.
CHW Change Hardware: indicates the contract under which Customer with a Permanent License (PL) without a PLSS subscription, shall have the right to Export said License (PL) from the computer on which such License was originally installed to another computer of its own. Customer is not entitled to the Software Support and Maintenance Services provided by the PLSS subscription contract.

Very important for the correct management of 'export of licenses' is what is provided in clause 1. DEFINITIONS - Export:


Transfer of a License from the computer on which it is currently enabled to another computer or to the same one if reconfigured, keeping the License unchanged. Export is allowed for one (1) time during the Subscription Period and only for Licenses with a PLSS, ASF, or ASP subscription.
