With Try & ... DPT allows its prospects and customers to evaluate the latest release for free for one week.


The complete name of the campaign depends on the target.


Try & ... Target
... Buy! Prospects, i.e. potential new customers
... Come Back! Customers with licenses 'out of maintenance' for a while
... Update! Active customers still using old versions.

Please note that Try & Come Back (to maintenance) portion of the campaign is the one with the biggest potential (BI Tools 2016).
